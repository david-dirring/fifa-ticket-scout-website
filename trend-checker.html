What you'll get

A real report. Broken down.

A friend of mine "won" a bunch of World Cup seats in the FIFA lottery and asked me how to price them. I started looking at the seats, the prices, the trends — and texted him back a small essay. Then I figured I could do the same for any seller, at scale, with the crowdsourced FIFA data I already had. So I built it. Below is what the report would've looked like, if my buddy from Boston was named Mark Wahlberg.
